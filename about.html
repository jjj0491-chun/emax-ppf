Cutting technology

The special construction for the special car is based on the version provided by the computer database, the paint mask is cut with a film cutting machine in advance, and then the paint mask is manually pasted

  • Save at least 1-2 meters of material per car, which can be used as interior film
  • The construction time of each vehicle is 6-8 hours, which improves the construction efficiency
  • More than 90% do not need to be demolished, eliminating construction risks
  • Higher accuracy, more beautiful edge. Cutting is accurate and does not require excessive stretching by technicians.
Traditional construction

The traditional construction technology is for workers to stick the paint mask to the car first, and then use a utility knife to cut the film manually.

  • Waste material, cost at least 2 meters of membrane material
  • Waste time, 1-2 days construction time
  • Use a knife to cut the film, high construction risk
  • Pay attention to the skills of technicians, and the barriers for film construction are high
