One week Notice After Installation

  1. Re-visit to our store for follow up review after one week.
  2. Do not drive with high speed within a week after installation completed.
  3. Do not wash car, caught in rain or in wet condition in the first week after installation completed. Can use towel to wipe clean oil stains, animal dirt or bird droppings or other corrosive substances on PPF surface but do not wipe the film edge.
  4. Edge peel off or small are bubbles is normal, but please send the car to our shop as soon as possible.

Completion Check Points

​Please refer to the following criteria to check the completed vehicle

  1. RFilm and paint surface between no obvious blister, hair, no dirty phase. Grain, one meter away do not show paint dust. Because space electrostatic dust can not be absolutely avoided, so the existence of a small number of very fine particles is normal. The inspection criteria may vary depending on the condition of the vehicle. After painting in the process of tearing film paint risk, please understand.
  2. The rounded corners of the film are regular and uniform. The packaging angle depends on the vehicle structure difference and the size of the special computer data.
  3. Early installation of the film surface may have fog, which is viscose moisture produced by the initial visual effects. Normal about ten days (affected by temperature and humidity) , fog disappear, the film surface appears mirror light.
  4. Early installation may produce membrane edge stress shrinkage caused by super-edge, dry evaporation of water bubbles, bubbles, above normal.
  5. Make sure to have a second inspection at the construction store one week after completion (before the first car wash) to ensure that the problem is resolved in a timely manner.
